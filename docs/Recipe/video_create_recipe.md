# Video Generation Recipe

Create and refine a short video based on your script, storyboard, or a high-level idea. The AWorld Agent turns your description into a generated video and can refine it over multiple steps.

## What You Get

- A short video generated from your prompt (e.g. target duration, scenes, or narrative)
- Support for script-, storyboard-, or idea-driven generation
- Iterative refinement based on your feedback or constraints

## Example: Space Exploration Video

**Prompt you can use:**

```text
Create a 30-second video about space exploration, starting with a rocket launch and ending with an image of Mars.
```

Adjust the topic, duration, and start/end scenes to match your idea. You can also provide a full script or storyboard for more control.

## How to Run

1. Install and configure AWorld-CLI (see the main [README](../../README.md)).
2. Launch the CLI and choose or enter the video-generation flow.
3. Paste or type your prompt (e.g. the one above), or attach a script/storyboard if supported.
4. Wait for the agent to generate the video; then review and request refinements if needed.

For installation and activation details, see [Your Journey with AWorld-CLI](../../README.md#your-journey-with-aworld-cli) in the README.
